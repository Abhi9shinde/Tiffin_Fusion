Data Deletion Instructions

If you would like to delete your account and personal data, please follow the instructions below:

How to Request Data Deletion

You can request your data deletion by either:

  • Sending an email to support@tiffinfusion.com.
  • Filling out the data deletion form in your account settings.

What Will Be Deleted?

The following data will be permanently deleted:

  • Your account information (name, email, etc.)
  • Your preferences and settings
  • Your personal data stored on our servers

Timeline

Once your request is processed, your data will be deleted within 30 days of your request.

Questions?

If you have any questions about the data deletion process, please contact us at support@tiffinfusion.com.
